George Brown College Casa Loma
Casa Loma Campus is located in the Casa Loma neighborhood of Toronto
The campus offers a variety of programs which range from certificates to diplomas and advanced diplomas. 
Some programs are tailored to the hospitality industry, taking advantage of Toronto's vibrant tourism scene.
The campus is equipped with industry-standard equipment and technology.
This includes specialized kitchens and labs for culinary arts and hospitality programs, classrooms, and computer labs.
One of the unique aspects of the campus is its proximity to the historic Casa Loma castle, a major tourist attraction in Toronto.
