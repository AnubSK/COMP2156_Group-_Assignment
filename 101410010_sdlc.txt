Importance of learning Devops Engineer in software development.
DevOps practices emphasize automation, continuous integration, and continuous deployment.
By implementing DevOps, organizations can release software updates more frequently and reliably.
This enable them to respond to market demands and customer feedback faster.
Promotes collaboration and communication between development, operations, and other stakeholders involved in the software delivery process. 
This alignment fosters a culture of shared responsibility, leading to more efficient workflows and better outcomes.
DevOps helps ensure consistency and reliability across development, testing, and production environments.
